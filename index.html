MOVE Conference is a one-day virtual missions conference.

February 25, 2021

Learn More

For the first time in the history of MOVE, we'll gather together online.

MOVE Conference is designed for anyone who is passionate about sharing the gospel locally, nationally and internationally. Pastors, church staff, college students and any leader who desires to learn, grow and develop in the area of missions will benefit from MOVE.

Register Now

Don't watch MOVE Conference alone!

We believe that the best way to experience MOVE is within a community that can help encourage each other, facilitate discussion, and challenge new ideas.

MOVE will be broadcasting live. Gather a group from your church, mission team, or college ministry and consider hosting a watch party. Group pricing is available for teams of 5 or more. Host kits will be mailed closer to the conference.
